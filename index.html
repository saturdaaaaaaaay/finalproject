Planilandia

How to play:

Explore the town and make new friends by completing their quests! To complete quests, look for items on the map and play games. Use the arrow keys to move around and interact with people or items. Click on the menu button to view the quest list or inventory.

Shortcuts:

Menu: "M"

Quests: "Q"

Inventory: "I"
